The Food Basket Project was initiated by Pastor Adeleke Adelani Coker. A missionary evangelist through the inspiration of the Holy Spirit in January 2017 when the Holy Spirit said to him through the scripture in Matthew 14:14, 16 & 20; ‘Jesus had compassion on them….and said to them give them something to eat….and they had more than enough’.
The Food Basket Project is aimed at providing the basic needs (such as food, medications, clothing materials, provision of books and payment of examination fees) for the needy especially those in the rural area.
The ultimate goal is to have a home and school for these set of people to keep and train them because people in need of these basic items keep increasing daily.
